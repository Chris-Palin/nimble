A little poem

Braver

❦

Comfort is a small warm room

with the curtains kindly drawn.

Courage is the cold brass latch,

the draught, the wider dawn.

Nothing soft was ever forged,

nothing sure was ever safe —

the trembling is not the warning,

the trembling is the way.

So stand where the ground is uncertain.

Let the discomfort come and stay.

Everything worth becoming

is waiting past the edge of afraid.
